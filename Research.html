Research Interests:

Development of novel biotherapeutics. We use a combination of chemical biology and synthetic biology approaches, including genetic code expansion, protein engineering, genome editing et al. to develop novel protein, virus and cell-based therapeutics.


Key Words:

Chemical Biology, Synthetic Biology, Biotherapeutics, Expanded Genetic Codes, Post-Translational Modifications, Antibody Engineering, Enzyme Engineering, Genome Editing, Supramolecular Chemistry etc.
